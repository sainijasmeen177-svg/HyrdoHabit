About HydroHabit

Your Ultimate Hydration Companion

HydrHabit is a premium, state-of-the-art hydration tracking web application designed to help you maintain a healthy lifestyle. Recognizing that adequate water intake is crucial for energy, focus, and overall well-being, we built a tool that is not only functional but beautiful and engaging.

By intelligently calculating your daily water needs based on your body weight, HydroHabit provides personalized goals. Our interactive dashboard features dynamic progress visualizations, historical charting to track your habits over time, and even a built-in BMI calculator for holistic health awareness.

Whether you're at your desk or on the go, HydroHabit is here to remind you that every drop counts towards a healthier you!

Why HydroHabit?

We calculate exactly how much water you need daily based on your weight and age.
